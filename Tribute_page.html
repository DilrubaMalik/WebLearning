Abir Chatterjee Early life:

Abir Chatterjee was born on November 18, 1980, in Kolkata, India, son of Phalguni and Rumki Chatterjee, like parent’s he engaged himself in the theater at early life. He was passionate to the film at his childhood than involved with an acting professing. Abir completed schooling from Kolkata, after schooling, he took admission for BBA degree in finance at Goenka College in Kolkata. Later for MBA degree in finance, he enrolled himself at the ICFAI University.

Abir Chatterjee Career:

Completing academic life in 2004, Abir Chatterjee joined in acting profession and he becomes a perfect actor in the showbiz. He started a career through the telefilms ‘Proloy’ which directed by Raj Chakraborty and aired on Sononda TV and ‘Shomoy’ telefilms also aired on Ruposhi Bangla Channel. ‘Proloy is the best telefilms of Abir Chatterjee and got popularity to the audience and producer as well as seen him on the big screen to do movies. He gets a chance within a short time in the big screen. Abir Chatterjee acted a lots movie and gained honor in the media industry. Cross Connection, Prem by Chance, Bedroom, Kanamachi and Jekhane Bhooter Bhoy are the romantic movies. Abir catches the audience’s mind by his romantic role, dialogue, and action. Bojhena Shey Bojhena is his best-acted movie, where he showed a terrific performance and gets acclamation from the viewers.
